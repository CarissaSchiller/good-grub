Cosy sweet potato bowl

Ingredients

1 small yellow onion

1 tablespoon vegetable oil

3 cloves garlic

1/2 teaspoon dried spices: cinnamon, turmeric, cumin, coriander, ground cloves

Salt, to taste

1 teaspoon fresh grated ginger

1 red chili

1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes

1 can (15 oz) chickpeas

1 large sweet potato

1 can (15 oz) light coconut milk

1/2 cup chopped parsley

Jasmine rice, for serving

Method

1. Soften onion with oil and garlic

2. Add spices and cook for several minutes

3. Throw in diced sweet potato, chickpeas and coconut milk

4. Turn down heat and leave to simmer for 40 mins

4. Serve with rice and enjoy!

Melt in The Middle Brownies

Ingredients

200 g quality dark chocolate (70%)

250 g unsalted butter

75 g raspberries, optional

50 g chopped nuts, optional

80 g quality cocoa powder

65 g plain flour

1 teaspoon baking powder

360 g caster sugar

4 large free-range eggs

Method

1. Preheat the oven to 180°C/350°F/gas 4. Line a 24cm square baking tin with greaseproof paper

2. Snap the chocolate into a large bowl, add the butter and place over a pan of simmering water, until melted, stirring regularly. Stir through the raspberries and nuts (if using)

3. Sift the cocoa powder and flour into a separate bowl, add the baking powder and sugar, then mix together

4. Add the dry ingredients to the chocolate, raspberry and nut mixture and stir together well. Beat the eggs, then mix in until you have a silky consistency

5. Pour the brownie mix into the baking tin, and place in the oven for around 25 minutes. You want them to still be gooey in the middle

6. Allow to cool in the tray, then carefully transfer to a large chopping board and cut into squares. Delicious served with a dollop of crème fraîche or yoghurt mixed with some orange zest
